Timeline

2001

I was born on September 27th in Kingston, Ontario.

2007

I moved from the suburbs to a more rural area.

2014

I participated in an exchange program with a school in Edmonton, Alberta. I had the opportunity to explore another part of Canada for the first time during the week.

2019

I graduated from high school, and after being rejected from the IMD program, I began working at McDonald's through a friend who held a manager position at the restaurant.

2021

I advanced to a swing manager position at McDonald's but declined a salary offer, as I realized food service wasn't my true passion.

2021

I moved from Kingston to Ottawa with my girlfriend because I knew my goal was to enroll in the IMD program, and staying in Kingston wouldn't help me achieve that.

2021

I began working at Mindgames as a part-time seasonal employee. Took some time to adjust from fast paced food service to customer focused and slower retail environment.

2022

I took a second job at McDonald's in Ottawa to negotiate a raise at Mindgames. I was offered a position as an assistant manager and helped set up a store in the Rideau Centre.

2023

Resigned from the assistant manager position to focus on my studies at Carleton University, pursuing a Bachelor of Arts.

2024

Secured a spot in the IMD program and accepted a position as Assistant Manager at a smaller Mind Games location.

2025

Built this website. Hi Max!
